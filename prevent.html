How is Childhood Cancer Prevented

Healthy Lifestyle

Healthy Lifestyle

Encouraging a healthy lifestyle can contribute to reducing the risk of cancer. This includes promoting a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while minimizing the consumption of processed foods. Encouraging regular physical activity and maintaining a healthy weight are also important.

Immunizations

Immunizations

Certain infections can increase the risk of developing specific types of cancer. Vaccinations against diseases such as hepatitis B and human papillomavirus (HPV) can help prevent some cancers associated with these infections.

Avoiding exposure to carcinogens

Avoiding exposure to carcinogens

Minimizing exposure to known carcinogens, such as tobacco smoke, radiation, and certain chemicals, is crucial. It is important to create smoke-free environments, limit sun exposure and use appropriate sun protection measures, and follow safety guidelines when handling hazardous substances.
